ratemycourse.com is a webpage For students who are sick of watching professors get dissed and would rather see people talk about courses. RateMyCourse aims to allow students from all universities rate their courses and give their thoughts on course outcomes. We believe that this site will benefit all students debating whether taking a course or not.
