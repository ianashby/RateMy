Type of people who use Rate my Course

Those who will use Rate my Course will be Students looking for a better way to get to know their school courses. they will be looking for workloads, average grades, whether they can skip, or any number of factors for their class.

Personas

Kyle Schofield

Age: 24
Major: Computer Science
Kyle is looking for a class that fill out his plan that wont be too difficult. In fact, the easier the better.

Maria Cruz

Age: 19
Major: Psychology
Maria is studying Psychology and wants to find electives that will support her in her career. Shes looking for a fun communication class.
